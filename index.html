Hello, and welcome to Parappa Party, your local PaRappa The Rapper and Um Jammer Lammy fansite! In here you can find all sorts of information regarding the PaRappa franchise, from development history to translations of Japanese-only interviews to fun facts about Katy Kat.

If this is your first time visiting this site or just want to lurk around without much of an actual reason, just start browsing this site by checking out the buttons on the sidebar, order doesn't matter.

We also have a Matrix chatroom for those who are interested, which you can access from the Chat button. It's like a Discord server, but cooler.

And if you want to see this site keep running for longer, you can donate some money to cover the server costs!

Regardless of intention or interest anyway, I hope you enjoy this site, and remember that it is always under construction. If you find out something is missing, check back later, and if it's still missing, then bug me on the Parappa.party chat about it.
This is Parappanon, signing out!
