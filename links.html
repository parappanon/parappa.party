Other PaRappa Sites

  • Whimsyload: Rodney Alan Greenblat's personal website, which contains a lot of information about not only PaRappa, but his other works across his life and various fields.
  • : The #1 place for PaRappa news and official content, straight out of Japan! It's been around for over 20 years now, and it even has a mailing list for anyone who wants to keep up with the latest PaRappa news (although in Japanese only).
  • : A nifty PaRappa page made to promote the anime that has, somehow, managed to survive to this day. It, however, quite literally cannot function without the now-outdated Flash, so you might want to grab Ruffle if you want to see this website the way it's intended to.
  • parappa-the-rapper.com[A]: A hub for the official PaRappa sites that were out there around 2001-2002. Unlike the Sony Music site, however, parappa-the-rapper.com has not managed to survive to this day, and its archive isn't exactly complete. Still a really good browse, though.
  • MilkCan.com[A]: A legendary site made to promote Um Jammer Lammy, with lots of cool and interesting stuff. It's available in both Japanese and English, too!
