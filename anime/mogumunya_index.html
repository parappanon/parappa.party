PJ Berri no Mogumogu Munyamunya is a short anime series that aired between 2016 and 2017, made to commemorate the 20th aninversary of the franchise and focusing on PJ Berri as it's main and namesake character.

The story revolves around his new role as the fortune teller of the Chop Chop Fruits Cafe, with him and PaRappa, who now works a a waiter who helps the customers around by listenIng to their problems.
At the same time, other episodes focus more on the daily life of PaRappa and PJ,

PJ Berri no Mogumogu Munyamunya aired on Fuji TV's late night show #Hi_Paul and had two seasons animated by Doga Kobo, with the second one being animated in Flash.
