Is it Scary?

  • Japanese Title: もしかして怖いの?
  • Scriptwriter: Akatsuki Yamayato
  • Storyboarder: Imura Morinama
  • Director: Yoshihisa Matsumoto
  • Animation Director: Yukio Ebisawa
  • Opening: LOVE TOGETHER by Nona Reeves
  • Ending: School Girl by Bernie K
  • Original Airdate: August 11th, 2001

Is it Scary? is the 12th episode of the Parappa the Rapper anime, written by Akatsuki Yamayato and directed by Yoshihisa Matsumoto.

Plot

The episode starts late at night in Parappa Town.
We see Sunny Funny walking across the street, but noticing that something's going on. She then turns around; and shocked at what she just saw, she lets out a loud scream.

The episode then cuts to a scene where Parappa falls off his skateboard just after the boys hear her. As they wonder what could have been the reason behind such a screen, a car passes through the street, and as Matt sees who's driving, he suspects it might be a monster who scared her off. The dog tells Parappa and PJ that there's been a lot of rumors surrounding sigthings of monsters all over PaRappa Town, scaring Parappa and making him tell Matt that he should stop. However, Matt reminds Parappa that it's just a rumor. The episode then goes back to Sunny, who realizes that the monster statue at the cinema she just looked at was just a statue.

At the same time, Katy is reading a book about ghosts at Paula's house when she starts hearing weird sounds seemingly coming from the window. Paula, who had just brought some stuff for them to get is told about this by Katy, and the vixen then reminds her that everything currently being told about the monsters are just rumors, opening the window in order to prove Katy wrong. Katy then decides to go to the bathroom, which leads Paula to snarkily tell her to stay safe because there are monsters out there. Despite the suspicions she's getting, Katy decides it's safe enough for her to go down the bathroom.

Parappa starts walking home, and soon enough starts getting scared by the things he sees around him; however, he soon calms down and remembers that the statue that scared him was just an object - just to sense that something might be going on.
As Katy goes down the bathroom, meanwhile; she starts wondering if there's a ghost around the house. She ends up feeling something, but doesn't think it was anything notable.
And Parappa doesn't thing anything seems out of the normal anymore.

The next day, Parappa tells his friends at school that the previous night was a scary one. As the gang starts discussing on how likely the rumors about monsters are to be true, Sunny comes into the classroom; but just as Parappa is about to tell her his opinons, Katy and Paula try to silence him, believing that it might attract them.

Night then comes again, and as Sunny walks through a park, she starts becoming unnerved due to the darkness and lack of people. The flower soon sits on a bench, but little does she know that the she is going to be kidnapped - once it happens, she lets out a scream loud enough for Paula and Katy to notice.
Parappa, who was just scared by a scene he saw on TV is soon after shocked by an incoming call from Paula, which Pinto responds for him.

Soon after, Parappa meets up with the rest of the gang at the park. Matt tells him he's late, but after asking the others where they think Sunny's located, Katy and Paula point to an ominous-looking castle on a hill, which will they now have to get into if they want to save Sunny.
As they start walking uphill, Paula tells him that there'll be monsters in the place, which causes Parappa to start imagining himself as a hero going to save Sunny, causing some rather dissappointed stares from PJ and Matt.

Once the gang is about to enter the haunted manor, however, Parappa suddenly becomes terrified and wants to go home. Despite that, his friends tell him that he cannot go back anymore, and as he remembers that Sunny is somewhere inside the manor he cries for a bit, but soon arms up enough courage to tell the others to go together - only to find out that he is being left behind.
After Parappa and friends enter the haunted house, they're mostly surprised by how empty yet spacious it seems.

As the gang explores the haunted house, they go through the rooms one by one. There doesn't seem to be anyone in the house, yet things seem to happen, such as when Parappa notices that a piece of meat in the kitchen is suddenly cute in half, or when the windows start flapping quickly, which scares everyone straight and makes then run away.

However, Parappa refuses to give up, and as he tells his friends that he wants to know where Sunny is, Sunny lets out a scream, which everyone recognizes.

This leads them to a big, lively dining room - which surprises the gang as they did not expect to see such a big party in a supposedly haunted house
A spider then shows sunny that it can create Sunny heads on its tongue, which scares Sunny and leads Parappa to tell everyone at the party to stop messing with Sunny - which surprises the creatures themselves, and makes the gang worried. Sunny, however, salutes them.

This leads PaRappa and friends to sit down for a meal, who are now asking for the reason behind why Sunny is in the haunted house.
The leader of the ghosts and monsters, who's a ghost that resembles Count Dracula then tells the gang that despite their constant attempts to scare people, they were always unfazed by the incidents they caused. This leads to a flashback showing that there were entities really behind all the incidents they thought were creepy or weird, but that they didn't think it was their doing at all. Sunny, however, was always scared by the monsters' actions, and that was the reason why they brought her into the party

After hearing the full story, the gang answers in various ways, but ultimately PaRappa decides to stand in front of them, only for Katy to come up with the idea of of giving them a makeover - as Paula soon starts applying makeup on them and Matt directs a synchronized dance to them.

To finish the episode, PaRappa and Sunny take a photo alongside some of the monsters they met at the party - the gang says cheese, PJ takes the photo and the resultws come out.
However, the final photograph scares Katy, Matt, Paula and PJ out of the house, surprising PaRappa, Sunny and the monsters.

Trivia

  • There was a 27 day break between the airing of Episode 11 and this episode. The reasons behind this extremely long break are unknown, but it is likely to have been a result of production issues and schedule shifting in favor of baseball games at the time.
