ACHO! ACHO!

  • Japanese Title: アチョ!アチョ〜!
  • Scriptwriter: Toshio Urasawa
  • Storyboarder: Takayuki Hamana
  • Director: Gahiko Tanaka
  • Animation Director: Yatsu Miyako
  • Opening: LOVE TOGETHER by Nona Reeves
  • Ending: School Girl by Bernie K
  • Original Airdate: June 30th, 2001

ACHO! ACHO! is the 13th epusode of the PaRappa anime, directed by Gahiko Tanaka and written by Toshio Urasawa.
This episode marks the first appearance of Chop Chop Master Onion in the PaRappa anime.

Plot

The episode begins with Matt and Paula visiting the Farmers' Market in PaRappa Town.

Main Characters

  • Katy Kat
  • Paula Fox
  • Pinto
  • Chop Chop Master Onion

Episode-exclusive Characters

  • Sweety Bancha

Trivia

  • Chop Chop Master Onion is the only character to be voiced by the same person in both the games and the anime, in this case Ryu Watanabe.
