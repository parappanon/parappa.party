PaRappa the Rapper TV Animation Soundtrack Volume 2

Japanese Name: パラッパラッパーテレビアニメーション サウンドトラックvolume.2

This album is the second of two CDs containing the various music tracks used in the PaRappa anime as its soundtrack, including the openings and endings. It was originally released by Sony under its Aniplex label on December 19, 2001, under a price of 2,913 yen with sales tax at the time, and a production number of SVWC-1317.
