PaRappa the Rapper Video Package

Like most anime, the PaRappa the Rapper anime recieved a home video release during and after the anime's airing. The PaRappa anime released in two formats: VHS and DVD, with the VHS releases being priced higher in order to encourage people to purchase the DVD version.

In addition to 3 to 4 episodes of the anime, every volume of the PaRappa anime's home release also contained a special complation of clips called "Special Stage" that featured a specific character and a character design sheet for this character.

PaRappa The Rapper TV Animation Stage.1

  • Release Date: October 24, 2001 (Japan)
  • Formats: VHS and DVD
  • Label: Aniplex
  • Price: 4800 yen (DVD)/7500 yen (VHS), excluding sales tax
  • Catalogue Number: SVWB-1531 (DVD)/SVWV-1351 (VHS)
  • Episodes Contained: Episodes 1 through 4
