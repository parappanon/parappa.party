Tommy Boy Presents: PaRappa The Party Mix

Mixed by DJ Kaori

A special album made in collaboration with the record label Tommy Boy in order to promote PaRappa The Rapper 2 and celebrate Tommy Boy's 20th anniversary as a record label.
It features tracks from both PaRappa The Rapper 2 and various artists who were signed with Tommy Boy at the time.
