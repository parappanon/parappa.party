PaRappa and PJ - I Scream!

Much like Make it Sweet!, PaRappa and PJ - I Scream!, which was released on September 12th of 1999 features remixed version of the songs from the Um Jammer Lammy Soundtrack, although in this case it is the melodies from PaRappa's versions of the game's songs sung by him alongside PJ, now talking about topics such as going to school, being nice to others and saying no to drugs.
