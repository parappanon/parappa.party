MilkCan - Make it Sweet!

Make it Sweet!, released in June 19th of 1999 is an album of remixed music from Um Jammer Lammy's soundtrack, with the main draw being that instead of the teachers singing the vocals it is Katy or even Lammy who's actually singing.

The album is musically styled in a way similar to a radio station, with fake commercials and instrumental tracks being placed between songs.
