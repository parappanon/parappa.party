PaRappa The Rapper 2 Original Soundtrack

Japanese Name: パラッパラッパー2 ― オリジナル・ サウンドトラック

This album includes all the tracks in PaRappa The Rapper 2, including but not limited to the title tracks, the background music used on the game's cutscenes, the playable songs in the game, the extra in-game tracks and a bonus remix as a bonus track.
It released in October 24, 2001, at a price of 2100 yen, and recieved versions in both CD and vinyl, like with PaRappa 1 before.
