RodneyFun Official Bio

Japanese

サニー・ファニーは明るくてとってもポジティブ。 ティーカップとAラインのワンピースをコレクションしているラブリー派。クッキング大好きな彼女はパスタやケーキ作りが大好きで、お友達を呼んでのティーパーティーもよく開きます。 ガーデニングは生活のキホンで、お花の世話は毎日の日課です。

Translated

Sunny Funny is spirited and very postive. She has a lovely collection of teacups and A-line dresses. A girl that loves cooking, she loves making pasta and cakes; calls her friends to her tea parties and often respond.
Gardening is the basis of her life, looking after the flowers every day her routine.

Appearance

Sunny Funny is an anthropomorphic flower, most resembling a cross between a sunflower and a daisy.
She has a yellow body, with her petals sprouting out of her head as if they were hair.
In her sprites and the anime, her eyes are blue, but in official art they're black like everyone else's.

She usually wears a one-piece dress with red and blue stripes alongside green shoes.

Personality

Sunny Funny's personality is quite incosistent between entries.

In the games, Sunny is shown to be a fairly mysterious person despite her importancy - while she's outgoing and confident, yet blunt - the game being from PaRappa's perspective means that we're kept in the dark regarding her for a long time.
It isn't until PaRappa 2's Come a Long Way music video where we get to see who really is - a kind girl who feels bad whenever the worst of her shows and works hard to keep her bonds agreeable.

In the anime, Sunny's personality is a lot more straightforward - she's cheerful, friendly, caring and hard-working, as well as the heart of the group, mediating whenever any of them gets in conflict with each other.

In the comics, her personality is shown to have traits of both, with an special emphasis on what she considers to be being polite - the secret to how she manages to be good at gardening and fishing.
In addition, despite her love of cooking, her plates are considered to be disgusting due to her ecletic choice of ingredients. However, her friends are always glad to go to her eating parties regardless.

Trivia

  • Sunny Funny does have a mother. She has only appeared in promotional material for RodneyFun collaborations, however, most notably the 1997 Waku Waku Tokyu one, where she's alongside General Potter in the background.
  • PaRappa has rejected her cooking at least twice - first in Sunny Funny's Polite Garden Party where she gives everything she serves him to the squirrels, then in PaRappa 2's intro after serving him noodles.
