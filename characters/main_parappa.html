Translated

Parappa's cool raps are his strong point. Any show of his raps and singing are like a natural-born rapper's. In Sunny-chan's dreams, he wants to become a strong hero who can capture her heart at any time he can think of. However, he dreads doing such a laborious effort. He's obssesed with console games, and is quite good when it comes to information about new games. PJ is his closest friend.

Phyisical Appearance

PaRappa is a small dog resembling a terrier or beagle.
He has cream or light yellow colored fur and black ears, which are usually colored with the same color as the outline of his body.
His signature headpiece is an orange beanie, which is noted to have an eliptic patch of a frog in a yellow background.

PaRappa has a variety of outfits, but his main one is a sleeveless shirt with blue baggy pants.

He almost always wears the same red sneakers with white waves shaped like the letter "M" on the sides, the only exceptions being formal ocassions and other situations where it wouldn't make sense for him to wear them.

He also has a pair of plaid pajamas with a matching hat, and he has been seen wearing other outfits in the 2001 anime, such as tuxedos and a karate uniform.

In PJ Berri no Mogumogu Munyamunya, he replaces his usual shirt for a long-sleeved one with white and navy horizontal sprites alongside a grey apron - switching over to a yellow one featuring the Chop Chop Fruits Cafe logo on the left front pocket.

Personality

Parappa is for the most part an outgoing and cheerful guy.

He's easily moved by the things that happen to him, but just as quick to pick up his own slack when there are problems to be solved.

Trivia

  • Parappa's species was not specific at first - considered species for him include a porcupine, a shrimp and an onion.
  • The original concept design of PaRappa as a dog was notably different from the final one - he wore a backwards cap and looked notably more smug.
  • His color scheme is different between official/promo art and the actual games - his fur color being more yellowish, his orangeorange hat more reddish and his shirt being indigo-colored as opposed to the sky-blue of the official illustrations. The anime uses the ingame color scheme.
  • If Katy's thought about him on Sunny Funny's Polite Garden Party is to be believed, Parappa is considered to be short even in-universe, which would likely make PJ and Sunny short as well.
