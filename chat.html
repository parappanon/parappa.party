Welcome to Parappa.party Chat

Parappa Chat is a publicly accessible chatroom and homeserver. It currently uses the Matrix protocol, which is akin to Discord, although decentralized as chats and accounts can be hosted in servers outside of Matrix's control.

If you have a Matrix account, you can directly join from there, but if you don't, we highly encourage you to either sign up at our homeserver, which uses the same domain as our site or to a recently updated homeserver in Join Matrix's list of public Matrix homeservers. This is in order to keep the Matrix network in good health.
In order to get the most functionality out of Parappa Chat, we recommend to use the client Nheko on desktop, and either FluffyChat or SchildiChat (Android only) on mobile devices.

If/Once you have a Matrix account, you can join Parappa.party's Matrix space below:

https://matrix.to/#/#matrix:parappa.party

Privacy Policy

Last updated in January 8th, 2023

  • Registration at the matrix.parappa.party homeserver is currently open. If abuse takes place, it will be closed and turn into an invite-only systen instead.
  • Guest access is currently enabled. If abuse happens, it will be disabled.
  • Federation is enabled.
  • Multiple settings have also been tuned for more stringent security, such as user_ips_max_age being disabled, bcrypt rounds being higher and nginx headers that could reveal IP addresses being disabled as well.
  • All sorts of metrics are disabled. However, we do keep some minimum logging, which last for about three days and are only useful for error logging.
  • We will NEVER sell or share your data to another party, no matter what.
  • Matrix federation may couse some information to be sent to third party servers due to how federation works, but we are not liable for such a thing.
  • If we get any sort of requests to share information with law enforcement, we will politely decline. We will resist against any further legal action unless it is absolutely necesary to do so.
